Distributed Cloud Summit gathers contributors, professionals, and researchers from cloud-native communities to share their idea for the next-generation cloud infrastructure using 5G, Edge and AI. It is an effort to motivate tech practitioners and aspirants to contribute to Open Source Distributed Cloud Infrastructure.

E-cell VNIT and Distributed Cloud Summit are collaborating to host a hackathon, which is an event where individuals or teams come together to work on and create innovative solutions to a specific problem or challenge. The hackathon will provide a platform for participants to showcase their skills, exchange ideas, and collaborate with others to develop creative solutions on topics 5G,Edge and AI
